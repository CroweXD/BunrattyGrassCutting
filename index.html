About Bunratty Grass Cutting

Hello, I'm Padraig Crowe, the proud owner of Bunratty Grass Cutting. Our company specializes in providing top-notch garden maintenance services, including meticulous hedge trimming, precise grass cutting, and a comprehensive range of other garden care services.

My extensive knowledge and experience in garden maintenance and landscaping services are the bedrock of Bunratty Grass Cutting and Garden Maintenance. This business is more than a profession to me; it's a passion driven by a deep love for transforming and nurturing gardens.

Based in Bunratty, Co.Clare, we extend our services to nearby areas, ensuring that exceptional garden care is just a call away for everyone in the region.

At Bunratty Grass Cutting and Garden Maintenance, we're committed to delivering high-quality gardening services at competitive prices. I personally guarantee the utmost care and high standards in maintaining your garden, ensuring results that not only meet but exceed your expectations.

Don't forget to check out our Facebook and Instagram pages for the latest updates and special gardening promotions.

Grass Cutting & Garden maintenence

Grass Cutting

Mowing not only maintains your lawn but promotes thicker, healthier growth by removing growth-suppressing tips from grass blades. We follow a smart mowing schedule, adapting to seasonal changes - bi-weekly in summer and less frequently in cooler months, ensuring optimal lawn health. Occasional leaving of grass clippings enriches your lawn with natural nitrogen, fostering a lush, green appearance. Our precision equipment is meticulously maintained for the highest quality cut. Trust us to give your lawn the expert care it deserves, all year round."

Garden Maintenence

We focus on nurturing your plants, flowers, and shrubs, ensuring year-round flourishing. We handle everything from pruning to soil health and seasonal planting, always with an eye for aesthetic harmony. Trust us to keep your garden vibrant and healthy, creating a perfect green retreat or a stunning floral display, no matter the season."
